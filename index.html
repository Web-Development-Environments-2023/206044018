About me:

My grandfather's first name is Michael and his last name is Hickri and he was born on July 23, 1942. His father's name was Reuven and his mother's name was Judith, and he was the only child of his parents. He grew up in the Nahaliel neighborhood in Hadera, which was a place with fruit trees, pine trees, sand and private houses. The thing that characterized the people of the settlement was that they were military men, agricultural workers and scholars. My grandfather lived with his grandparents in a private house with 1 floor and 7 rooms, and there were other rooms that were rented out to people. The conditions in his home were excellent, his grandfather was a butcher and they always had plenty of meat, and their yard was full of fruit trees and vegetables. He studied at Bilo state-religious school in Hadera and was in a youth movement called Gadna, where he trained militarily and athletically. He was really tall and had a lot of friends. In addition, he was the painter of the school and he painted huge paintings and pictures from the Bible. My grandfather's family used to be together on holidays and all the relatives came to their house. On vacation days, the family used to travel.
He remembers the authentic Yemeni food he ate when he was a child and on Saturdays he and his family would sit together under the trees in the yard to eat fruit, crack kernels and tell stories.

Main differences between web 1.0, web 2.0, web 3.0:



First difference - Features:
Web 1.0 - the page is static, not dynamic - "you get what you see".
Web 2.0 - more dynamic, the user creates content.
Web 3.0 - dynamic and using AI and Natural language processing to create a personal experience for each user.

Second difference - Content:
Web 1.0 - mostly used for companies to post information that people consume.
Web 2.0 - more collaborative, social and there are different platforms where people can post content that other people can consume.
Web 3.0 - people build apps that other people can interact with and companies build platforms that enable people to advertise services by encourage people-to-people relationships.

Third difference - How to search:
Web 1.0 - The user writes the link itself for search.
Web 2.0 - The user searches by write the name of the search engine and get results that literally contain the words.
Web 3.0 - The user searches by write the name of the search engine and get results that contain the words or semantically related to the search words.

Use case of search for specific remedy:

web 1.0 - The user need to input specific URL, Need to know where the site of drugs is through a portal, then search the page of specific drug and at the page need physically look for what is needed.

web 2.0 - The user write in the search engine the desired drug, then the user get the results that includes this words and have to physically go through the results and check which ones are relevant.

web 3.0 - The user write in the search engine the desired drug, the search engine is with intelligent AI that understands the context.
Then the user get the results that contain the words or semantically related to the search words.
In addition there were summarize of some of the pages. Therefore, the user knows that the returned documents are relevant to what he is looking for and it is easier for him to decide.
